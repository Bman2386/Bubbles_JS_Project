The health of your bubble is its size. If you collide with any object except a powerup, your bubble loses health & if your bubble is at 1 health you pop (game over). If your in shield mode, your protected against all enemies and you have a boost ability (by tapping space while moving) Shield mode lasts about 6 seconds (this time limit resets each time you pick up a shield). If your score gets high enough you'll face the boss and if you defeat the boss you win!
